Nio Jin is a communication designer raised in Beijing. They are currently based in New York, pursuing a BFA in Communication Design at Parsons School of Design.  Nio Jin is a digital alchemist constructing poetic systems where logic simulates life and interaction becomes a ritual of perception. Their practice begins in dissolution: a quiet rejection of clarity, utility, and surface logic. They do not take meaning for granted. They dismantle it—gently, precisely, and with intent. What appears static may evolve; what seems direct may loop. Their systems resist passive viewing. Instead, they quietly demand participation—offering neither clear entry points nor promised answers. Meaning is never delivered; it is discovered—through doubt, detour, or play. Nio is drawn to slow resonance, perceptual slippage, and the thrill of decoding meaning through experience.
